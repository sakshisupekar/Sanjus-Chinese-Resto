About Us

Welcome to Sanju's Chinese Resto, where authentic Chinese flavors meet a warm, inviting atmosphere. Nestled in the heart of Satara, we take pride in serving traditional recipes crafted with love and the finest ingredients.

Our journey began with a passion for sharing the rich culinary heritage of China. Each dish on our menu is a celebration of regional flavors, from the bold spices of Sichuan to the delicate aromas of Cantonese cuisine.

At Sanju's Chinese Resto, we believe in more than just food—we strive to create memorable dining experiences. Whether you're savoring our signature dim sum, slurping a bowl of hot and sour soup, or indulging in our crispy Peking duck, every bite tells a story of tradition and taste.
